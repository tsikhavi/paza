about paza studios

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to offer creative, quality and reliable services to the media, entertainment and corporate world as we work towards becoming one of the leading Audio and Video studio brands in Kenya and the region. 

"Consistency in producing excellent audio and visual content is the best ​possible preparatio​n for success."

Who we are

Paza Studios is a one-stop, industry-standard audio and video recording facility that is equipped with the newest technology . Before changing its name, PAZA STUDIOS began operations in Kenya in 2008 under the name "ABH Sound" to offer music production and sound engineering services. We have , over the years, established ourselves in a position to work with a diverse clientele, ranging from solo musicians to corporate entities. In addition we work with Kenya's film industry by creating original music, mixing & mastering as well as postproduction. Lastly, we create commercial jingles and other relevant services for businesses. 
Find out more.
